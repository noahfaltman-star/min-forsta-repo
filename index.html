Bugatti Vision Gran Turismo

The Bugatti Vision Gran Turismo is a single-seater, one-off concept car that served as a design precursor to the Bugatti Chiron. Unveiled at the 2015 Frankfurt Motor Show, it features a heavily modified quad-turbocharged W16 engine, all-wheel drive, and an extreme aerodynamic design inspired by Bugatti's racing heritage and LMP1 cars.
